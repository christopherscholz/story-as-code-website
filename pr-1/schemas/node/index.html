Node Schema

An entity in the world graph.

Typeobject
RequiredNo
Additional propertiesAny type allowed

Description: An entity in the world graph — characters, locations, objects, events, or any custom type.

Example:

id: hero
name: Aria
type: CHARACTER
description: A young warrior chosen by prophecy to restore balance to the realm. Raised
  in obscurity, she discovers her lineage only after the fall of the capital, and
  must reconcile her quiet upbringing with the violent destiny thrust upon her.
properties:
  title: The Chosen One
  age: 22
  homeland: The Northern Reach
  affiliation: Order of the Silver Dawn
tags:
- protagonist
