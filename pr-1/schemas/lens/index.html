narrative layer

Lens Schema

Narrative perspective — filters and interprets world content.

Typeobject
RequiredNo
Additional propertiesAny type allowed

Description: A narrative perspective — who tells the story, what they know, and how reliable they are.

Examples:

id: hero-pov
perspective:
  type: CHARACTER_BOUND
  anchor: hero
  person: FIRST
knowledge:
  mode: CHARACTER_KNOWLEDGE
voice:
  vocabulary_level: COLLOQUIAL
  sentence_tendency: SHORT_STACCATO
reliability:
  level: SELECTIVE
  omits:
  - secret-meeting

id: omniscient
perspective:
  type: OMNISCIENT
  person: THIRD
knowledge:
  mode: OMNISCIENT
voice:
  vocabulary_level: LITERARY
  metaphor_density: HIGH

11.2. Property conflict_resolution

Typeobject
RequiredNo
Additional propertiesAny type allowed
PropertyPatternTypeDeprecatedDefinitionTitle/Description
- knowledgeNoenum (of string)No-UNION — knows everything any sub-lens knows. INTERSECTION — only shared knowledge. PRIORITY_WINS — higher priority decides.
- emotionNoenum (of string)No-BLEND — averaged. PRIORITY_WINS — higher priority decides. DOMINANT — strongest emotion wins.
- voiceNoenum (of string)No-PRIORITY_WINS — higher priority decides. ALTERNATING — switches with active sub-lens.
- reliabilityNoenum (of string)No-LEAST_RELIABLE — inherits least reliable. MOST_RELIABLE — inherits most reliable. PRIORITY_WINS — higher priority decides.

11.2.1. Property knowledge

Typeenum (of string)
RequiredNo
Default"PRIORITY_WINS"

Description: UNION — knows everything any sub-lens knows. INTERSECTION — only shared knowledge. PRIORITY_WINS — higher priority decides.

Must be one of:

  • “UNION”
  • “INTERSECTION”
  • “PRIORITY_WINS”

11.2.2. Property emotion

Typeenum (of string)
RequiredNo
Default"BLEND"

Description: BLEND — averaged. PRIORITY_WINS — higher priority decides. DOMINANT — strongest emotion wins.

Must be one of:

  • “BLEND”
  • “PRIORITY_WINS”
  • “DOMINANT”

11.2.3. Property voice

Typeenum (of string)
RequiredNo

11.2.4. Property reliability

Typeenum (of string)
RequiredNo
